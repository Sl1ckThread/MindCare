Meet Dr. Eko Gustiawan

Dr. Eko Gustiawan is a highly respected mental health professional with over 15 years of experience in the field of psychiatry and psychology. With a deep understanding of the unique pressures faced by university students, Dr. Eko specializes in helping young adults navigate the complexities of academic life and personal development.

At MindCare, Dr. Eko Gustiawan is dedicated to providing personalized care and practical solutions to help you achieve mental wellness and academic success. His commitment to fostering a supportive and understanding environment ensures that you have the tools and guidance needed to thrive both in and out of the classroom.
